      Covariance and Correlation

      Before we dive into our conclusion, let's go over the meaning over covariance and correlation. Covariance indicates how two variables are related. In the case of our project it is Subjectivity and Polarity. In our case, we have a positive covariance. Let's move on to correlation, correlation is another way to determine how two variables are related. Basically it will tell us whether variables are positively or inversely related and the degree to which the variables tend to move together.

      Take a look at the chart above. The covariance between the two variables is 0.0701026. This indicates that the variables change in the same direction. The correlation is 0.82298. This is an extremely high correlation considering the value is above 0.5 and very close to 1.0.
